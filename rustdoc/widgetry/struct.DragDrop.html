pub fn new(ctx: &EventCtx<'_>, label: &str, axis: StackAxis) -> Self

This widget emits several events.

  • Outcome::Changed(label) when a different card is selected or hovered on
  • Outcome::Changed("dragging " + label) while dragging, when the drop position of the card changes. Call get_dragging_state to learn the indices.
  • Outcome::DragDropReleased when a card is dropped

When you build a Panel containing one of these, you may need to call ignore_initial_events(). If the cursor is hovering over a card when the panel is first created, Outcome::Changed is immediately fired from this widget.
