Expand description

Transform the Seattle-wide Endpoints into specific TripEndpoints for this map. When the endpoint happens to be a building on the map, this is straightforward. Otherwise, the endpoint will snap to a border intersection.

When only_passthrough_trips is true, only trips beginning and ending off-map are returned. When it’s false, all other trips are returned.
